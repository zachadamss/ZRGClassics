About ZRG Classics

Building a resource hub for the classic German car community

Our Mission

ZRG Classics started as a place to buy and sell classic German cars. We've since evolved into something bigger: a community-driven resource hub dedicated to helping enthusiasts maintain, repair, and enjoy their classic Porsches and BMWs.

We believe that the knowledge needed to keep these machines running shouldn't be locked behind paywalls or scattered across dozens of forums. Our goal is to consolidate the collective wisdom of the enthusiast community into one accessible place.

What We Offer

Vehicle Resources

  • Common issues and their symptoms for each platform
  • Recommended fixes with parts sourcing information
  • Attribution to original sources and community contributors

Coming Soon

Contribute
